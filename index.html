Garbage University

The time was running out and each of us wanted to get to Garbage University. But unfortunately they chose only the best ones...

Garbage University was famous for its mentors who were made from first-class garbage. Everyone who entered this university returned as a garbage collectors.

REAWAKEN YOUR CREATIVITY

Community

Garbage Friends community is another important component of a fulfilling life. Discovering new visions that share your passions and growing with them creates a real sense of meaning. Garbage University will be a community that cares about its vision of the world and encourages it becoming better.

Creativity

We believe that every person was born with creative potential. When we turned 3, our imagination, curiosity and creativity were at their peak. As we grow up, it is brought up with us. Creativity has no limits. Even when you think it's just a trash, others can see an art. We call them Garbage Collectors. They were accepted to the university with new knowledge, expanding it.

Collaboration

Great things happen when we work together. A true collaboration is when multiple parties combine to create something greater than just individual parts. Challenging each other to experiment in new ways creates unexpected results - and innovation comes from the unexpected.
